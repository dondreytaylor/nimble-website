What is a full node?

A node is device with software responsible for verifying consensus rules and relaying transactions (usually in the form of blocks) of a particular blockchain.

Running a full node is the only way to interact with a blockchain in a trustless way. Full nodes are also secure and do not suffer from the same attacks that could occur with light wallets.

Furthermore, having the entire blockchain downloaded is the most private way store, send, and receive crypto funds.

What is nimbleNODE?

nimbleNODEs are plug-in-play chain agnostic full nodes that can be controlled anywhere from a mobile app. Unlike traditional full nodes that need to download an entire blockchain before they can be used, nimbleNODEs come with a verifiable pre-image of the blockchain of your choosing.

Do I need to be technical to use nimbleNODE?

No. nimbleNODE abstracts out all the technical complexity of setting up a full node and makes the process as easy as connecting to WiFi and powering up. nimbleNODES can be fully controlled from any iOS or Android phone.
